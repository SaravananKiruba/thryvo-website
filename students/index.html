Frequently Asked Questions

Is Thryvo free for students?

Yes, our basic platform is free for all students. Premium features like advanced courses and one-on-one mentorship may have associated costs.

How do I get access to campus placements?

Once you create your profile, you'll get access to placement drives happening at your college. You can also explore off-campus opportunities based on your skills and preferences.

What skill courses are available?

We offer courses across various domains including programming, data science, digital marketing, design, soft skills, and more. New courses are added regularly based on industry demands.

Can Thryvo help with international applications?

Absolutely! We provide resources for standardized tests, university selection, application preparation, visa guidance, and scholarship applications for global education opportunities.

Do you provide internship opportunities?

Yes, we connect students with both summer internships and part-time opportunities throughout the academic year across various industries.
